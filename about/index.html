ABOUT


Madsir Studio is a collection of works by artist and developer H. B. Bird.


GAMES



Creating video games full of radness and madness since 2019.

Current Releases:

Calculation Boogey | Solve the math problems before the sun sets or the boogeyman will get you.

Space Toots | Eat space jellies, get gas, and propel your way through the spacescapes. Beware! the meteors and gravity effectors ready to knock you off course.

Chinchilla Dance Party | These chins are so excited about math they decided to throw a dance party! Solve the math problems to keep their party going.



ASSETS



Developing digital assets for adding creative aspects to games since 2021.

Current Releases:

Cutscheme (2D/3D) | Cutscene Tool: Easily create and control cutscenes. Infinite camera shots. Can control multiple cutscenes in one scene.

Utterer | Multi-Tool: Easily add dialogue and captions to any game. Includes localization framework, dynamic string interpolation, and .csv/.txt import/export features.

Earchestra | Sound Manager/Playlist Creator: Create and store infinite audio playlists. Access all of them from any script in the scene by using Earchestra's Maestro Methods.


ARTWORK



Bird's current works focus on 2D and 3D environmental concepts. Certain works can be found in this site's portfolio.
Digital: Conceptual artwork, 2D assets, 3D models, textures, sprite sheets, cG shaders, animation, materials, and much more.

Traditional: Oil and acrylic paintings. (Not displayed in current Portfolio)
