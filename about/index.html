ABOUT


Madsir Studio was established in 2019 and represents a collection of works by Artist, Game Developer, and Digital Asset Creator H. B. Bird.



GAMES



As a Game Developer, Bird has handled the conception, design, development, and launch of three multi-platform games using Unity and Android Studio.



ASSETS



As a Unity Asset Publisher, Bird has developed and designed multiple digital tools for use in game development. These tools are crafted to make it easier for game developers to add creative aspects like dialogue, cutscenes, and audio into their games.



ARTWORK



As an Artist, Bird has over a decade of experience creating digital and traditional artwork. Bird's current portfolio focuses on digital 2D and 3D environmental concepts.

Other forms of artwork by H. B. Bird include 2D & 3D art assets (concepts, backgrounds, UI, textures, tiles, sprite sheets, etc.), technical designs (shaders, materials, GUI, etc.), and traditional oil and acrylic paintings.

Currently accepting commissions for digital works only.
