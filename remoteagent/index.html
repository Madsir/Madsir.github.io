Remote Agent [In Production]

A strange chaos spreads across the land, causing a remote monitoring station to lose all contact.

In this demo:

Play through the first levels of this adventure game.

Collect banana slugs and energy balls for extra lives.

Collect enough in each level and you'll be rewarded with a bonus round cheat codes.

Any cutscenes or elements that could spoil the storyline for the full game have been removed.
