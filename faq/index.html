How did you come up with the idea for Remote Agent?

Heard a story about an old lady picking up NATO signals on her landline. I don't know how it turned into this but it did.


How did you come up with the idea for Chinchilla Dance Party?

I have dance party breaks with my chinchilla (I dance, he popcorns) while I'm programming and one of those days was full of math and he was super excited so I made a game out of it.
