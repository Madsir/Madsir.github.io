SPACE TOOTS



Coming Soon!




Spatoots has an insatiable appetite for space jellies.




These jellies cause an abundance of toots.







Jump through black holes to travel across the universe in search of jellies.


Get spit out into a jelly-filled spacescape.







Walk, jump or toot through the spacescapes.


Eat as many jellies as possible.







At the end of the level is another black hole.


Jump through it to complete the level.







Beware!

These spacescapes contain meteoric hazards.



Time-altering and gravity-altering effectors can be helpful or harmful.







Features four main levels.


Completing a level unlocks the next.







Completing all four levels unlocks the Space Toots Generator.


This generator is capable of generating thousands of unique spacescapes.
