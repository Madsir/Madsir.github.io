Spatoots has an insatiable appetite for space jellies. These jellies cause an abundance of toots which can be used for propulsion.










Travel across the universe in search of space jellies. Jump through black holes into new, jelly-filled spacescapes.









Walk, jump or toot through the spacescapes. Eat as many jellies as possible.









Beware! These spacescapes contain meteoric hazards that can knock Spatoots off course. Spacescapes also contain time-altering and gravity-altering effectors which can be helpful. Or harmful.









Each spacescape contains a black hole. Jump through it to end the level and head into the next spacescape.









Features four spacescape levels. Completing a level unlocks the next. Completeing all four levels unlocks the Space Toots Generator.








Enter any number with seven digits or less as the seed for the Space Toots Generator to generate a unique and new spacescape to explore. This generator is capable of generating thousands of unique spacescapes.
