Chinchilla Dance Party




Solve the math problems to help these chinchillas keep their party going.

This game is played using a mouse.




Categories:

Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division.




Levels:

Nine in total. Three are unlocked from the start, but all can be unlocked by spending stars.

Beat time challenges to gain stars.




Timed Challenges:

Answer correctly ten times in a row within a challenge time period.

The challenge time period is determined by the difficulty setting you choose. (Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert)

The higher the difficulty, the more stars you get for beating the challenge.
