Chinchilla Dance Party is a mathematics-practice game with groovy music and dancing chinchillas.

Do the math and help these chins keep their party going!



Categories: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division.

Features nine levels. Three are unlocked from the start, but all can be unlocked by spending stars.

Stars are gained by answering correctly ten times in a row within a challenge time period.

The challenge time period is determined by the difficulty setting you choose. (Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert)

The higher the difficulty, the more stars you get for beating the challenge.




Easy instructions.
Nine levels, nine songs.
Choose a math category.


Beat challenges for stars.
Stars unlock new levels.
Saves your highest score.
