Race against time to solve basic math problems so you can keep the music playing and the chinchillas dancing!




Contains nine levels, each with their own unique environment and original song.


Features Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division.


Choose between four levels of difficulty. Beat time challenges to earn stars and use those stars to unlock new levels. Saves your highest score.
