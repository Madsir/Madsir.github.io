Cutscheme Cutscene Tool


Easily create cutscenes which can be activated automatically, triggered by collision, or enabled after a set period of time.

All cutscenes will automatically disable themselves after finishing or if skipped.

This tool works by building a sequence of camera shots in the scene and giving you control over each shot's position, rotation, duration, and transition as well as audio and visual effects.

Includes visual effects systems for Built-In & URP pipelines.

Cutscheme Controller

Control a camera (or parent of a camera) in the scene.







Cutscheme Object Control
Hide or show objects before, during, and after the cutscene



Objects hidden before cutscene will be automatically disabled in the editor, though can be enabled and disabled manually.

Objects hidden during the cutscene are hidden at the start of the cutscene and enabled again after the cutscene.

Objects hidden after the cutscene are disabled after the cutscene ends.




Cutscheme Audio
Controls the main audio (if any) for the cutscene.



Loop the cutscene audio and control the volume from Cutscheme Controller.

Automatically fades the main audio in and out.

Option to only play audio originating from cutscene.




Cutscheme Options



Fade the cutscene in/out at the beginning and end.

Reset the camera(s) back to their original sizes after the cutscene ends.

Reset the camera(s) back to their original transforms after the cutscene ends.

Choose whether the cutscene can be skipped.

Change to a new scene via build index or let the Cutscheme prefab disable itself when the cutscene ends.




Camera Shots
Controls for each camera shot :



Position of each camera shot.

Rotation of each camera shot.

Duration the camera shot stays still before transitioning to the next.

Zoom in or out to a new FOV at the speed of your choice.

Shake the camera at the magnitude of your choice.

Play an audioclip at the start of the camera shot.

Speed up or slow down time during the camera shot.

Transition to the next shot by cutting instantly or panning/rotating at the speed of your choice.






Cutscheme VFX Systems



Cutscheme Effect System
(Universal Render Pipeline only)



Display multiple camera effects during each shot in the cutscene.

Copies the volume you drag into the system and uses the copy to create and control effects for the cutscene.

After the cutscene it resets your volume back to normal and removes the copy.







Cutscheme Filter System
(Built-In Renderer Pipeline only)



Display up to two camera filters at a time during each shot in the cutscene.

Customize with your own filters (Materials) or use the ones that come with the package.
