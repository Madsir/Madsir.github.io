Cutscheme is a handy cutscene tool for creating custom cutscenes, cueing cutaway camera angles, triggering cinematic transitions, and much more!

Cutscheme uses your input to build a sequence of camera shots in the Editor, and at Runtime directs your camera through the sequence to create your cutscene.

Customize each shot's position, rotation, duration, and transition. Manage audio, apply effects, enable skipping, and more all from one component. No coding necessary.

Use pre-built Cutschemes or easily create custom cutscenes which can be activated automatically, triggered by collision, or enabled after a set period of time.
