REMOTE AGENT | DEMO


Completely reworked and updated this demo.

It is one level long and gives a feel for what the first half of the game is like.

Updated enemy mechanics so they can work together to fight you
Updated Rooster's animation to include falling & added more banana slugs to collect for life
Increased the effects of special attacks
Added more chaos and monsters at every turn

It's still a work in progress but definitely getting there. Let me know what you think!
