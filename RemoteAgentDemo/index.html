*Please note: Although this demo is still in production, I think showing what this game is like is better than describing it with words. Remote Agent Demo: Intro + 3 levels.


Controls:
Move left: A or left arrow
Move right: D or right arrow
Jump: W or up arrow. Press twice for double jump.
Slide: S or down arrow while walking left or right.
Hack with ax: L-Shift or R-Shift
Throw: Space or Keypad Enter
Pause: Z, then any other key to resume

*I will be continually updating this demo as time goes on, so stay tuned!
