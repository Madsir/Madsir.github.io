CUTSCHEME 2D | CUTSCENE SYSTEM



Easy-to-use "plug and chug" cutscene tool designed for use with orthographic cameras.


The Cutscheme 2D system syncs with each Cutscheme 2D prefab added to a scene and can control multiple Cutscheme 2D prefabs in the same scene, though only one can be enabled at a time during runtime. It automatically adds or removes camera positions, effects, etc. to the scene based on your input in the Cutscheme 2D prefab's Cutscheme 2D Controller.



Cutscheme 2D Controller:
-Control a camera (or parent of a camera) in the scene. (Cutscheme 2D will automatically detect whether the transform you drag into the Main Camera field is a Camera or a parent of a Camera and will reformat itself accordingly. This is so Cutscheme 2D can be used for top-down games as well.)
-Allows option to show or hide outlines of the camera positions in the cutscene. These outlines reflect each of their respective camera sizes and positions. Idle camera positions are outlined in dark purple and selected camera positions are outlined in light green.


The Cutscheme 2D system is designed to be your camera crew, handling the technical aspects so you can play director and let your creativity flow.

Create standalone cutscenes, opening cutscenes, triggered cutscenes, transition cutscenes and trailers.

This system works with top-down, side-on, and isometric camera styles.
