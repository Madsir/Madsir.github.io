Cutscheme 2D is designed to be your camera crew, handling the technical aspects so you can play director and let your creativity flow.

Easily create standalone cutscenes, opening cutscenes, triggered cutscenes, transition cutscenes, and more!

For orthographic cameras only (top-down, side-on, isometric, etc.)

Available on the Unity Asset Store


Cutscheme 2D Controller
Control a camera (or parent of a camera) in the scene.





Cutscheme Object Control
Hide or show objects before, during, and after the cutscene



Objects hidden before cutscene will be automatically disabled in the editor, though can be enabled and disabled manually.

Objects hidden during the cutscene are hidden at the start of the cutscene and enabled again after the cutscene.

Objects hidden after the cutscene are disabled after the cutscene ends.




Cutscheme Audio
Controls the main audio (if any) for the cutscene.



Loop the cutscene audio and control the volume from Cutscheme 2D Controller.

Automatically fades the main audio in and out.




Cutscheme Options



Fade the cutscene in/out at the beginning and end.

Reset the camera(s) back to their original size(s) after the cutscene ends.

Choose whether the cutscene can be skipped.

Change to a new scene via build index or let the Cutscheme 2D prefab disable itself when the cutscene ends.




Camera Positions
Controls for each camera position :



Duration the camera position stays still before transitioning to the next.

Zoom in or out to a new camera size at the speed of your choice.

Shake the camera at the magnitude of your choice.

Tilt the camera at the angle of your choice.

Transition to the next position by cutting instantly or panning at the speed of your choice.

Play an audioclip at the start of the camera shot.

Speed up or slow down time during the camera shot.






Cutscheme VFX Systems



Cutscheme 2D Effect System
(Universal Render Pipeline only)



Display multiple camera effects during each shot in the cutscene.

Copies the volume you drag into the system and uses the copy to create and control effects for the cutscene.

After the cutscene it resets your volume back to normal and removes the copy.







Cutscheme 2D Filter System
(Built-In Renderer Pipeline only)



Display up to two camera filters at a time during each shot in the cutscene.

Customize with your own filters (Materials) or use the ones that come with the package.
