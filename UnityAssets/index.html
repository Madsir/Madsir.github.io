Coming Soon - Cinemat2D Cutscene System



A straightforward and easy to use cutscene tool for Unity 2D.

How it works:


Syncs with each Cinemat2D prefab added to a scene. Can control multiple Cinemat2D prefabs in the same scene, though only one can be enabled at a time.
Automatically adds or removes camera positions, effects, and objects in the scene based on your input.
Choose whether the cutscene can be skipped
Change to a new scene or let Cinemat2D prefab disable itself upon end of cutscene
Add a filter to the camera for the entire cutscene (vignette, black&white, etc.)
Controls for each camera position:
-duration of the shot before transitioning to the next
-zoom in or out to a new camera size at the speed of your choice
-shake the camera at the magnitude of your choice
-tilt the camera at the angle of your choice
-cut instantly or pan at the speed of your choice to the next position
-add filter to the camera for this shot (vignette, black&white, stc.) ((overrides main filter))
