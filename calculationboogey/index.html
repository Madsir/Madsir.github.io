Calculation Boogey




Solve the math problems before the sun sets or the boogeyman will get you.




Categories:

Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division.




Levels:

Nine levels for each category. Each level has five rounds.




Rounds

Solve the required number of equations in each round. Beat 5 rounds to advance to the next level.
