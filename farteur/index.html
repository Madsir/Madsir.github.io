FARTEUR







The farteur has an insatiable appetite for space jellies. These jellies cause an abundance of flatulence.










The farteur jumps through black holes to travel across the universe in search of jellies.


The farteur gets spit out into a jelly-filled spacescape.







Walk, jump or fart-propel through the spacescapes and eat as many jellies as possible.








At the end of the level is another black hole.


Getting near its gravitational pull will drag the farteur through, completing the level.







Beware! These spacescapes contain meteoric hazards as well as time-altering and gravity-altering effectors.









Features four main levels. Completing a level unlocks the next.








Completing all four levels unlocks the Farteur Generator, capable of generating thousands of unique spacescapes.
