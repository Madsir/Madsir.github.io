FARTEUR







The farteur has an insatiable appetite for space jellies. These jellies cause an abundance of flatulence. Eat space jellies, get gas, and let 'er rip through the spacescapes.









Beware! These spacescapes contain meteoric hazards as well as time-altering and gravity-altering catalysts.









Features four main levels. Completing a level unlocks the next.








Completing all four levels unlocks the Farteur Generator, capable of generating thousands of unique spacescapes.








Enter and exit spacescapes through black holes.
