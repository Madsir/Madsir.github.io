FARTEUR



The farteur has an insatiable appetite for space jellies. These jellies cause an abundance of flatulence.







Propel your way through spacescapes and consume space jellies for fart fuel.

Beware! These spacescapes contain meteoric hazards as well as time-altering and gravity-altering catalysts.

Features four main levels. Completing a level unlocks the next.

Completing all four levels unlocks the Farteur Generator, capable of generating thousands of spacescapes.





Enter and exit spacescapes through black holes.
